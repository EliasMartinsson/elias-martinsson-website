Intermediates - Suitable for trainees with experience of 3 months - 1.5 years

Push

Suitable for intermediates in the gym who have started to decrease their recovery time. The push variant of the ppl split targets the pectorial muscles, fronterior and lateral deltoid muscles and the heads of the tricep.

Pull

Suitable for intermediates in the gym who have started to decrease their recovery time. The pull variant of the ppl split targets the entire back and bicep muscles which are activated through "pulling-movements". These include lats, posterior deltoids, traps and more.

Legs

Suitable for intermediates in the gym who have started to decrease their recovery time. The legs variant of the ppl split targets the lower body, especially the quadriceps, hamstrings, abdominal muscles and calves.
